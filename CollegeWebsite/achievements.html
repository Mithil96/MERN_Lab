Achievements

PVPSIT students have consistently excelled in academics, research, and innovation.

In 2022, PVPSIT was ranked 98th by Outlook, 130th by Business World, 141st by The Week, and 142nd by India Today.

Students have won prizes in various events at different colleges. For example, in 2022, Ch. Bhavya Srujana won first prize in the singing contest and Sk. Farid Baba and Team won first prize in the skit contest.

The department has formed four Quality Circles to enhance quality education. These circles include skill training, industry institute interaction, and centers of excellence.

The department has been accredited by the NBA three times, in 2007, 2012, and 2016, and twice more in 2019 and 2022. The department has state-of-the-art infrastructure and computing equipment.
